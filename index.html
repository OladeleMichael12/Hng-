My Tech Goals for the Next 2 Years

Over the next two years, I aim to achieve significant milestones in my journey as a web developer. First and foremost, I plan to master the core front-end technologies: HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. To accomplish this, I will complete ten projects that showcase various aspects of front-end development, such as responsive design, animations, and interactivity.

In addition to mastering the basics, I intend to gain expertise in a popular front-end framework like React.js or Vue.js. I will build five complex applications using the chosen framework, contribute to open-source projects, and complete a comprehensive course or certification to solidify my knowledge.

Expanding my skills to back-end development is also a priority. I will become proficient in a back-end language such as Node.js, Python, or Ruby. This goal will be achieved by completing three full-stack projects where I handle both front-end and back-end development and obtaining a relevant certification. Furthermore, I will deepen my understanding of databases by designing and implementing five projects that utilize SQL and NoSQL databases like MySQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB.

Improving my workflow and tooling is essential for efficiency. I aim to become proficient in using Git and GitHub by regularly employing these tools in all my projects and contributing to ten open-source projects. Additionally, I will learn advanced Git features to enhance my version control skills. Understanding the basics of DevOps and Continuous Integration/Continuous Deployment (CI/CD) is another goal. I will set up a CI/CD pipeline for at least two projects using tools like Jenkins, GitHub Actions, or GitLab CI.

Enhancing my problem-solving skills is crucial for my development as a web developer. I plan to solve one hundred problems on platforms like LeetCode, HackerRank, or CodeSignal to improve my algorithm and data structure knowledge. Building a personal brand is also important to me. I will establish myself as an authority in web development by writing twelve blog posts, creating a portfolio website, and presenting at two local meetups or conferences.

Networking and community involvement are key components of my professional growth. I aim to grow my professional network by joining three professional groups or online communities, attending five conferences or webinars, and actively participating in discussions. Additionally, I plan to develop and launch at least three personal projects, deploying them on platforms like Vercel, Netlify, or AWS.

Contributing to open-source projects is another goal. I will make meaningful contributions to three projects on GitHub that interest me, contribute regularly, and get at least one pull request merged. Finally, I will stay up-to-date with the latest trends and technologies in web development by allocating five hours a week to continuous learning, following industry blogs, and taking online courses on platforms like Coursera, Udemy, or freeCodeCamp.

By setting and working towards these goals, I am confident that I will make significant strides in my career as a web developer over the next two years.
